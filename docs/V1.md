AntiTrend - V1 Definition

1️⃣ What problem does V1 solve?
Reduces exposure to fast-fashion and ultra-fast commerce sites so users can shop in alignment with their values.

2️⃣ Who is it for right now?
Online shopper who wants to avoid fast fashion and commerce

3️⃣ What does it block or filter exactly?
Unwanted sites that fall under 3 categories: fast fashion, ultra-fast fashion, and fast commerce

4️⃣ What does it NOT do in V1?
AntiTrend does not judge users, track behavior, or push ideology.

5️⃣ What does a user experience in the first 60 seconds?

User selects which categories to block.
Downloads or activates the block list.
Begins browsing with those sites filtered.
